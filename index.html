About Vimana

We Build More
Than Structures

Founded with a vision to redefine construction standards in Karnataka, Vimana Construction has grown into one of Bengaluru's most trusted full-service firms. Over 26 years and 450+ projects — from intimate luxury renovations to landmark commercial developments.

Our philosophy: every project deserves obsessive attention to detail, transparent communication, and a relentless commitment to quality. We don't outsource critical work. Our in-house team of certified structural engineers, architects, and project managers ensures every build is delivered on time, on budget, and beyond expectation.

Every new build comes with a 10-year structural warranty — because we stand behind every nail, beam, and finish we produce.
